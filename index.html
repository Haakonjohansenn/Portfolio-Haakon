About Me

Hello, my name is Haakon Johansen, a 26-year-old living in Oslo. Currently, I am pursuing my studies in front-end development at Noroff School of Technology and Digital Media. I recently completed my first year, and so far, I am thoroughly enjoying this journey!

What captivates me the most about coding is the endless array of solutions it offers to solve problems. It's like solving a complex puzzle, and I find great satisfaction in discovering new approaches and expanding my knowledge every day.

Beyond coding and work, I am an avid sports enthusiast, with football being my primary passion. I also enjoy hiking, camping, and embracing the wonders of the outdoors. And of course, nothing compares to the joy of spending quality time with my friends and family.

I am excited to showcase my portfolio and share my journey as a front-end developer. If you have any questions or would like to connect, feel free to reach out. Let's embark on this exciting adventure together!
